The app that helps you stay safe in a rapid changing world

How important is it for you to ensure or to know if your child or your own staff is safe in case of a serious incident?

The iFlash mobile application will be your key for survival and provides a solid communication platform that never fails to deliver. The app bundles all necessary in case of emergency communications into one closed channel making it safer and more effective.

Just one last step You´re entering a safer world.iFlash comes with:

Total privacy

Geolocation module used only during the event. We promise to never sell or use the data for any other purpose than keeping you safe.

Controlled communication

iFlash is an alarm and communications platform that helps you navigate through emergencies. Communicate encrypted with phone, IM and SMS.

Event management

iFlash comes with authorization and presence management. Use it for softer happenings and authorize personnel taking your children to events.

Group

Developed within the Swedish data security industry it offers a secure communication in closed communities. Use it for Family, Class and Fellowship groups.

Easy to manage GUI

The App comes fully equip with an easy to use graphical interface and includes Contact management, Dashboard and Group Admin.

One tap away from staying safer

What it if there are unauthorized personnel in school or one child missing from day care? Don’t gamble! Get iFlash today and communicate safer.

Built with the user in mind

iFlash is built for the user and aspires to be the safest communications channel on Earth. Your connection is fast and reliable wherever you live and wherever you go.
